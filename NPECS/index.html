New Paltz Extreme Croquet Society

The New Paltz Extreme Croquet Society is a club dedicated to playing croquet and having a good time. Neither snow nor rain nor heat nor gloom of night stays these croquet enthusiasts from doing what they love. We play in all terrain and weather throughout the SUNY New Paltz campus. The club is a member of the United States Croquet Association and members attend an annual national collegiate tournament at the Merion Cricket Club in Pennsylvania where schools from across the country compete.
